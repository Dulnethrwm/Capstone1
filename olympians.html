Michael Phelps

Michael Phelps is arguably the greatest Olympian ever. He has the most Olympic gold medals and numerous other accolades.

  • Michael Jordan: Basketball legend.
  • Usain Bolt: 8-time Olympic gold medalist.
  • Mark Spitz: 7 gold medals in a single Olympics.
  • Nadia Comaneci: Achieved a perfect 10 in gymnastics.
  • Paavo Nurmi: Finnish track and field star.
  • Lance Armstrong: Cycling champion.
  • Larisa Latynina: 9 gold medals in gymnastics.
  • Ian Thorpe: Australian swimming legend.
  • Jackie Joyner-Kersee: Heptathlon record breaker.
